Rohit Sharma

Height: 5'7" (170 cm)
Education: Swami Vivekanand International School, Mumbai

Early Life and Background

Rohit Sharma was born into a middle-class family in Nagpur, Maharashtra. His father, Gurunath Sharma, worked as a caretaker at a transport firm, and his mother, Purnima Sharma, is a homemaker. Financial constraints during his early life meant that Rohit was raised by his grandparents and uncles in Borivali, Mumbai. He was later admitted to a cricket academy thanks to his uncle's financial support, which marked the beginning of his cricketing journey.
Rohit's prodigious talent became evident during his school days. His coach, Dinesh Lad, recognized his potential and encouraged him to switch from off-spin bowling to batting. The decision proved crucial to his career.

Domestic Career

Rohit Sharma made his domestic debut for Mumbai in 2006 and quickly established himself as one of India's most promising young cricketers. His performances in domestic tournaments like the Ranji Trophy and Deodhar Trophy caught the selectors' attention.

Personal Life
