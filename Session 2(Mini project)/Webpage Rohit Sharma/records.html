Rohit Sharma

Records

Rohit Sharma is celebrated for his elegant batting style, impeccable timing, and ability to score big hundreds. His ability to play under pressure and deliver match-winning performances has earned him the nickname "Hitman." As a captain, he is lauded for his tactical acumen and calm demeanor.

Rohit's achievements have solidified his place among the legends of Indian cricket, and his contributions to the sport continue to inspire aspiring cricketers worldwide.

International Career

ODI Debut: June 23, 2007, against Ireland
T20I Debut: September 19, 2007, against England
Test Debut: November 6, 2013, against West Indies
Rohit Sharma initially struggled with consistency in international cricket but found his true calling as an opener in ODIs and T20Is. His promotion to the top order in 2013 was a turning point in his career.

ODI Highlights

  • First Double Century: Rohit scored 209 runs against Australia in 2013.
  • Three Double Centuries: Holds the record for the most double centuries in ODIs, including a world-record 264 against Sri Lanka in 2014.
  • 2019 Cricket World Cup: Scored five centuries, the most by any batsman in a single edition.

T20I Highlights

  • One of the most successful batsmen in T20I history, known for his explosive hitting.
  • Has four T20I centuries, the most by any player.

Test Career

  • Although initially overlooked in Tests, Rohit found success as a Test opener, scoring a double century against South Africa in 2019.

IPL Career

  • Rohit Sharma is one of the most celebrated players in the Indian Premier League (IPL). He captains the Mumbai Indians (MI) and has led them to multiple IPL titles.
Achievements
  • Under his captaincy, MI won the IPL in 2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, and 2020.
  • Rohit is among the top run-scorers in IPL history.
