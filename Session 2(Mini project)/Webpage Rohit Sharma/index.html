Rohit Sharma

Overview

Rohit Sharma, born on April 30, 1987, in Nagpur, Maharashtra, is one of the finest cricketers in the world and a key figure in Indian cricket. Known as the "Hitman," he is famous for his stylish batting, explosive power, and ability to score big runs. Rohit holds the record for the highest individual score in ODIs (264 runs) and is the only player to score three double centuries in the format. He is also one of the most successful T20I batsmen, with four centuries to his name.

Rohit captains the Indian cricket team across all formats and has earned acclaim for his calm and strategic leadership, leading India to titles like the 2023 Asia Cup. As captain of the Mumbai Indians in the IPL, he has achieved unmatched success, guiding the team to a record five IPL championships.

Rohit Sharma's journey, marked by consistency, leadership, and elegance, makes him an inspiration and one of cricket's all-time greats.
