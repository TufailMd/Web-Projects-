About me

My journey into coding began during my early teenage years when I was captivated by problem-solving and the logic behind computer programs. This passion has only grown over the years as I delved deeper into programming languages, algorithms, and building projects from scratch.

Web Developer

Internship Aspirations

My short-term goal is to secure an internship where I can contribute to impactful projects, and my long-term aspiration is to work at a leading MNC, building innovative software solutions.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy playing football and cricket, exploring books like Atomic Habits, and spending time honing my interpersonal and communication skills. My journey so far has been a blend of curiosity, hard work, and an unending drive to learn and grow.
